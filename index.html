About Our Dojang

Building Champions
On & Off the Mat

Moe's Sidekicks has been transforming lives through the art of Taekwondo for over two and a half decades in the Merced and Atwater communities. Founded by Master Jason Moe, our dojang teaches both World Taekwondo (WT) and International Taekwondo Federation (ITF) styles -- blending traditional Korean martial arts with modern training methods.

Our programs are built around developing the whole person: mind, body, and spirit. With approximately 70% kicking techniques and 30% hand techniques, students develop incredible athleticism while learning life skills that carry far beyond the dojang.

Whether you're 4 years old or an adult, a complete beginner or an experienced martial artist, we have a program designed for you. Join our family and discover what it means to be a Sidekick.

Meet Your Instructor

Master Jason Moe

Head Instructor & Founder

Master Jason Moe has dedicated over two and a half decades to teaching Taekwondo and transforming lives in the Merced and Atwater communities. His passion for martial arts and commitment to his students has built Moe's Sidekicks from the ground up into the award-winning program it is today.

Training in both World Taekwondo (WT) and International Taekwondo Federation (ITF) styles, Master Moe brings a comprehensive approach to instruction. His philosophy centers on developing the whole person -- building character, confidence, and community alongside physical skills.

Master Moe is deeply invested in community safety and youth development, regularly organizing adventure camps, community events, and awareness programs that extend the values of the dojang into everyday life.

Senior Brown Belt

Decided rank demonstrating advanced proficiency at the Brown Belt level. Students master all Brown Belt curriculum and begin preparing for Red Belt.

Red Belt

The Red Belt signifies danger and mastery approaching. The student has gained significant skill and must demonstrate control, humility, and leadership on the path to Black Belt.

Red White

The final decided rank before Black Belt. The student must demonstrate complete mastery of all curriculum, exemplary character, and readiness for the responsibilities of a Black Belt.

Red/Black Belt

The Bodan rank -- the final step before Black Belt. This belt represents the transition from red to black, symbolizing that the student stands at the threshold of mastery and must prove they are ready.

Black Belt

The culmination of all knowledge, discipline, and spirit. A Black Belt is not the end -- but the beginning of a new cycle.
